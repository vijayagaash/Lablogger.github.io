Welcome to Lab Logger!

Lab Logger is a comprehensive web-based tool designed to streamline and enhance the management of laboratory data and experiments. Whether you're a researcher, technician, or lab manager, Lab Logger offers a user-friendly interface to track, document, and analyze your scientific work with ease.

Select a Lab

  • Click to Select: Click on the name of the lab you wish to work with. This will open the lab’s detailed view.
  • Lab Details: Review the lab’s details including active projects, current data, and team members.

Access Lab Resources

  • Projects: View or manage ongoing projects within the selected lab.
  • Templates: Access lab-specific templates for data entry and documentation.
  • Reports: Generate or review reports related to the lab’s activities.
